          GOLIBE FESTIVAL

          The Golibe Festival Onitsha is a programme of the Onitsha 21st Century project which targets to leverage our rich cultural heritage to transform Onitsha into a preferred destination for culture, tourism and a hub for modern day commerce and innovation.

          CITY OF ONITSHA

          Onitsha is a city located on the eastern bank of the Niger River, in Nigeria's Anambra State. A metropolitan city, Onitsha is known for its river port and as an economic hub for commerce, industry and education.
